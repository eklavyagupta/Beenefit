REGISTRATION SECTION

You must be registered to keep bees in Victoria. Click here to start registration

Cost (up to 5 hives): free registration online; $30 for paper application.

Cost (more than 5 hives): 6-50 hives:$30 ; 60 cents / hive for more than 50 hives.


Hint: After successfully registered, you will receive a certificate which lasts for 2 years. After that, please renew your registration.

You can click here to get more beekeeper registration information form VIC government

HIVE DENSITY CHECK SECTION

If density of hives meets the requirements, you can start beekeeping on Victorian land without a planning permit.

Urban areas:

Tenement size Max No. of hives
500 ㎡ or less 1
501 ㎡ to 1000 ㎡ 2
1001 ㎡ to 2000 ㎡ 5
2001 ㎡ to less than 4000 ㎡ 10
4000 ㎡ to less than 1ha 60

OBTAIN BEES SECTION

Two methods:

1.You can buy queen bees from a queen breeder from September until the end of February.

2.Collect spring and early summer colonies. To collect bees, locate a box beneath the swarm and shake the branch, to further move bees left over, use smoker.

EQUIPMENT PURCHASE LIST

· Beekeeper’s smoker

· Protective clothing, beekeeper’s veil

· Robber gloves (bees hates dark colour gloves)

· Australian hive tool (“J” hook)/ American hive tool

· Langstroth hives
