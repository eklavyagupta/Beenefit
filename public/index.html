REGISTRATION SECTION

You must be registered to keep bees in Victoria. Click here to start registration

Cost (up to 5 hives): free registration online; $30 for paper application.

Cost (more than 5 hives): 6-50 hives:$30; 60 cents / hive for more than 50 hives.


Hint: After successfully registered, you will receive a certificate which lasts for 2 years. After that, please renew your registration.

QUEEN BEES OBTAINING SECTION

There are two ways to do that:

1.You can buy queen bees from a queen breeder from September until the end of February.

2.Also, you can collect bees in wild by yourself. To do that, you can locate a box beneath the swarm and shake the branch to let the bees fall into the box. The smoker can be used for further collecting the left-over bees.

DISCLAIMER: Please be fully aware of the danger of collecting wild bees by yourself.
