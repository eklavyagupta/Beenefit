Seasonal Activities of Bees

Bee activities are seasonal throughout the year,
so we provide a timeline with activities for beekeepers to conduct conrresponsively based on the bee activities.

  • Bee Activities 1. Queen egg production increases due to longer daytimes.
    2. The population of bees grows as the brood nest growing.
    3. Swarming might happen.
    Beekeeper Activities     1. Recommended that 9-14 day interims for inspection.
    2. Purchasing queen bees from queen breeders if needed.
    3. Avoid swarming:
    1) Avoid congestion: provide more room before building the hive or add more honey super.
    2) Provide To ventilation: Drill holes in the honey supers and don’t cover the ventilation hole.
    Spring
    Sep - Nov
  • Bee Activities · Queen's egg production raise
    · Possibility reduced for bees to raise queen cells for coming swarming
    · Keeping large brood nests and bee numbers increase
    Beekeeper Activities     · If there is surplus honey, collect it!
    · Buy queen bee from queen breeders if you need
    · Inspect hives every 3-6 weeks
    ·Cater bees with a stack to make them have enough room to build combs
